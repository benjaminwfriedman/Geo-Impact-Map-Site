About the Project

Hello everyone. My name is Benny. Thanks for checking out my maps! Geo Impact used to be a company that I founded focused on creating better urban data and models for the world. However, like many great ideas...it didn't work. So now I am just using the name and the skills I learned through starting the company to make interesting maps for people like you to look at! I don't know where this is going to go, but hopefully somewhere cool. Thanks again for checking it out.
