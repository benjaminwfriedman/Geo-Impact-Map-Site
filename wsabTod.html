WSAB Network Based TOD Zones

Los Angeles

The Los Angeles Metro has begun scouting a project to build a new metro line extending from east Downtown Los Angeles to Artisia, CA, using existing unused rail right of ways. This analysis uses as network based service area analysis to identify areas around each proposed Metro Stop, within a half mile walk. Though rough, this provides a good idea of areas that could be included in a new TOD zone or at a minimum, would benifet by the greater access provided by the new line.
